# 3. 屯 Zhūn — Difficulty at the Beginning / Sprouting

**Korean** — 수뢰둔 (水雷屯)
**Trigrams** — Water (坎) over Thunder (震)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `100010`

## Judgment
Sprouting works sublime success through perseverance. Do not act lightly; helpers further.

## Image
Clouds and thunder — the noble one brings order out of confusion.

## Interpretation
A seed pushing through hard soil. Beginnings are messy; resistance is part of the package. Gather allies, move slow.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Hesitation, hindrance — appoint helpers.
- **Line 2** — Difficulties pile up — wait ten years to marry.
- **Line 3** — Hunting deer without a guide — give up the chase.
- **Line 4** — Horse and wagon part — seek union, all goes well.
- **Line 5** — Difficulty blessing — small things, perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Horse and wagon part — bloody tears flow.
