# 6. 訟 Sòng — Conflict / Arguing

**Korean** — 천수송 (天水訟)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Water (坎)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `010111`

## Judgment
In conflict, sincerity meets obstruction. Caution brings good fortune; carrying it through brings misfortune.

## Image
Heaven and water move in opposite directions — the noble one considers the beginning before any undertaking.

## Interpretation
Heaven over water, moving apart. Friction inevitable, victory empty. Mediate, retreat, or settle — pushing for a 'win' loses anyway.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Don't perpetuate the affair — small talk, all ends well.
- **Line 2** — Can't engage in conflict — return and yield.
- **Line 3** — Nourished by ancient virtue. Follow the king without claiming credit.
- **Line 4** — Turn back to one's true nature — change attitude, find peace.
- **Line 5** — Conflict before the great person — supreme good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Granted a leather belt only to have it stripped three times before day ends.
