# 53. 漸 Jiàn — Development / Gradual Progress

**Korean** — 풍산점 (風山漸)
**Trigrams** — Wind (巽) over Mountain (艮)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `001011`

## Judgment
Development. The maiden is given in marriage. Good fortune. Perseverance furthers.

## Image
On the mountain, a tree — the noble one abides in dignity and virtue, in order to improve the mores.

## Interpretation
One step at a time, the right way, in the right order. Like a wedding procession — each stage must complete before the next. Skipping kills it.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — The wild goose gradually draws near the shore. The young son is in danger. There is talk. No blame.
- **Line 2** — The wild goose gradually draws near the cliff. Eating and drinking in peace and concord. Good fortune.
- **Line 3** — The wild goose gradually draws near the plateau. The man goes forth and does not return. The woman carries a child but does not bring it forth. Misfortune. It furthers one to fight off robbers.
- **Line 4** — The wild goose gradually draws near the tree. Perhaps it will find a flat branch. No blame.
- **Line 5** — The wild goose gradually draws near the summit. For three years the woman has no child. In the end nothing can hinder them. Good fortune.
- **Line 6** — The wild goose gradually draws near the cloud heights. Their feathers can be used for the sacred dance. Good fortune.
