# 16. 豫 Yù — Enthusiasm / Providing-For

**Korean** — 뇌지예 (雷地豫)
**Trigrams** — Thunder (震) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000100`

## Judgment
Enthusiasm — useful to install helpers and set armies marching.

## Image
Thunder bursts forth from earth — kings of old made music to honor merit, offering it to the supreme deity.

## Interpretation
Energetic readiness, the kind that lifts a crowd. Channel it into shared purpose. But check: is the buzz real, or are you just hyped on vibes?

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Enthusiasm expressed — misfortune.
- **Line 2** — Firm as a rock — not even a whole day. Perseverance, good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Enthusiasm that looks upward — remorse. Hesitation brings remorse.
- **Line 4** — Source of enthusiasm — great things are attained. Doubt not.
- **Line 5** — Persistently ill but never dying.
- **Line 6** — Deluded enthusiasm. But if after completion one changes, no blame.
