# 61. 中孚 Zhōng Fú — Inner Truth

**Korean** — 풍택중부 (風澤中孚)
**Trigrams** — Wind (巽) over Lake (兌)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `110011`

## Judgment
Inner truth. Pigs and fishes. Good fortune. It furthers one to cross great water. Perseverance furthers.

## Image
Wind over lake — the noble one discusses criminal cases in order to delay executions.

## Interpretation
When you're rooted in truth, even the dullest creatures sense it. Influence by inner alignment, not technique. Listen, then speak from the still center.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Being prepared brings good fortune. If there are secret designs, it is disquieting.
- **Line 2** — A crane calling in the shade. Its young answers it. I have a good goblet. I will share it with you.
- **Line 3** — He finds a comrade. Now he beats the drum, now he stops. Now he sobs, now he sings.
- **Line 4** — The moon nearly at the full. The team horse goes astray. No blame.
- **Line 5** — He possesses truth that links together. No blame.
- **Line 6** — Cock-crow penetrating to heaven. Perseverance brings misfortune.
