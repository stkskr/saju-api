# 36. 明夷 Míng Yí — Darkening of the Light

**Korean** — 지화명이 (地火明夷)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Fire (離)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `101000`

## Judgment
Darkening of the light. In adversity it furthers one to be persevering.

## Image
The light has sunk into the earth — the noble one, when associating with the great mass, conceals their light yet still shines.

## Interpretation
Hostile environment. Hide your brilliance to survive. Keep the inner flame burning while playing dim on the outside. This passes.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Darkening of the light during flight — he lowers his wings. The noble one does not eat for three days. Wherever he goes, people gossip.
- **Line 2** — Darkening of the light injures him in the left thigh. He gives aid with the strength of a horse. Good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Darkening of the light during the hunt in the south. Their great leader is captured. One must not expect perseverance too soon.
- **Line 4** — He penetrates the left side of the belly. One gets at the very heart of the darkening of the light, and leaves gate and courtyard.
- **Line 5** — Darkening of the light as with Prince Chi — perseverance furthers.
- **Line 6** — Not light but darkness. First he climbed up to heaven, then he plunged into the depths of the earth.
