# 49. 革 Gé — Revolution / Skinning

**Korean** — 택화혁 (澤火革)
**Trigrams** — Lake (兌) over Fire (離)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `101110`

## Judgment
Revolution. On your own day you are believed. Supreme success. Furthering through perseverance. Remorse disappears.

## Image
Fire in the lake — the noble one sets the calendar in order and makes the seasons clear.

## Interpretation
Time for radical change. Old skin off, new one forming. Don't act on impulse — wait for the day, then move with conviction. People will trust the sincerity.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Wrapped in the hide of a yellow cow.
- **Line 2** — When one's own day comes, one may create revolution. Starting brings good fortune. No blame.
- **Line 3** — Starting brings misfortune. Perseverance brings danger. When talk of revolution has gone round three times, one may commit oneself, and people will believe.
- **Line 4** — Remorse disappears. People believe them. Changing the form of government brings good fortune.
- **Line 5** — The great person changes like a tiger. Even before they question the oracle, they are believed.
- **Line 6** — The noble one changes like a panther. The inferior person molts in the face. Starting brings misfortune. To remain persevering brings good fortune.
