# 1. 乾 Qián — The Creative / Force

**Korean** — 중천건 (重天乾)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111111`

## Judgment
The Creative works sublime success, furthering through perseverance.

## Image
Heaven moves with strength; the noble one makes themselves strong and untiring.

## Interpretation
Pure creative force — six unbroken yang lines. The moment of pure potential and primal initiative. Time to act with integrity, but stay grounded; unchecked yang burns out.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Hidden dragon. Do not act — your time hasn't come.
- **Line 2** — Dragon appears in the field. It furthers one to see the great person.
- **Line 3** — All day active, alert at night. Danger, but no blame.
- **Line 4** — Wavering flight over the depths. No blame.
- **Line 5** — Flying dragon in the heavens. Meeting the great person furthers.
- **Line 6** — Arrogant dragon will have cause to repent — pulling back is wisdom.
