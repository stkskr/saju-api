# 58. 兌 Duì — The Joyous / Lake

**Korean** — 중택태 (重澤兌)
**Trigrams** — Lake (兌) over Lake (兌)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `110110`

## Judgment
The joyous. Success. Perseverance is favorable.

## Image
Lakes resting on one another — the noble one joins with friends for discussion and practice.

## Interpretation
Double lake — open, communicative, mutually enriching joy. The right friends sharpen and lift you. Joy founded in truth, not flattery.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Contented joyousness. Good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Sincere joyousness. Good fortune. Remorse disappears.
- **Line 3** — Coming joyousness. Misfortune.
- **Line 4** — Joyousness that is weighed is not at peace. After ridding oneself of mistakes, one has joy.
- **Line 5** — Sincerity toward disintegrating influences is dangerous.
- **Line 6** — Seductive joyousness.
