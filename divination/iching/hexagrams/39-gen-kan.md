# 39. 蹇 Jiǎn — Obstruction / Limping

**Korean** — 수산건 (水山蹇)
**Trigrams** — Water (坎) over Mountain (艮)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `001010`

## Judgment
Obstruction. The southwest furthers. The northeast does not further. It furthers one to see the great person. Perseverance brings good fortune.

## Image
Water on the mountain — the noble one turns their attention to themselves and molds their character.

## Interpretation
Walking with a limp. Obstacles ahead, energy not yet right. Turn inward, fix yourself, then move. Bring helpers.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Going leads to obstructions; coming meets with praise.
- **Line 2** — The king's servant is beset by obstruction upon obstruction — but it is not their own fault.
- **Line 3** — Going leads to obstructions — hence one turns back.
- **Line 4** — Going leads to obstructions; coming leads to union.
- **Line 5** — In the midst of the greatest obstructions, friends come.
- **Line 6** — Going leads to obstructions; coming leads to great good fortune. It furthers one to see the great person.
