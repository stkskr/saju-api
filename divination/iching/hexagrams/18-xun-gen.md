# 18. 蠱 Gǔ — Work on the Decayed / Correcting

**Korean** — 산풍고 (山風蠱)
**Trigrams** — Mountain (艮) over Wind (巽)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `011001`

## Judgment
Work on what has been spoiled — supreme success. Furthering to cross great water. Before the starting point, three days; after the starting point, three days.

## Image
Wind blows low on the mountain — the noble one stirs up the people and strengthens their spirit.

## Interpretation
Something rotted under your inheritance — family pattern, old project, neglected mess. Time to clean it up. Slow, patient, generational work.

- **Line 3** — Setting right what father has spoiled — slight remorse, no great blame.
- **Line 4** — Tolerating what father has spoiled — going on brings humiliation.
- **Line 5** — Setting right what father has spoiled — one meets with praise.
