# 2. 坤 Kūn — The Receptive / Field

**Korean** — 중지곤 (重地坤)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000000`

## Judgment
The Receptive brings sublime success through the perseverance of a mare. Following furthers; leading misleads.

## Image
The earth's condition is receptive devotion; the noble one carries the outer world with breadth of character.

## Interpretation
Pure receptive force — six broken yin lines. Response, support, and nurturance. Power here comes from going second, not first. Yield, follow the flow, and capacity expands.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Hoarfrost underfoot — winter is coming. Notice early signs.
- **Line 2** — Straight, square, great. Without effort, nothing remains undone.
- **Line 3** — Hidden talent. Serve the work; let credit fall where it will.
- **Line 4** — Tied sack. No blame, no praise. Discretion now.
- **Line 5** — Yellow lower garment brings supreme good fortune. Quiet authority.
- **Line 6** — Dragons fight in the meadow — their blood is black and yellow. Disaster from overreach.
