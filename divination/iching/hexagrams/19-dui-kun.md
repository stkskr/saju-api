# 19. 臨 Lín — Approach / Drawing Near

**Korean** — 지택림 (地澤臨)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Lake (兌)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `110000`

## Judgment
Approach has supreme success. Perseverance furthers. When the eighth month comes, there will be misfortune.

## Image
The earth above the lake — the noble one is inexhaustible in teaching, boundless in tolerance and protection of people.

## Interpretation
A great wave of growth is approaching. Window of opportunity opening. But the I Ching warns: take it now; the same energy reverses in time.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Joint approach — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Joint approach — good fortune, everything furthers.
- **Line 3** — Comfortable approach — nothing furthers. If one is induced to grieve, no blame.
- **Line 4** — Complete approach — no blame.
- **Line 5** — Wise approach — this is right for a great prince. Good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Greathearted approach — good fortune, no blame.
