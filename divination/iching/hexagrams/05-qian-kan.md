# 5. 需 Xū — Waiting / Attending

**Korean** — 수천수 (水天需)
**Trigrams** — Water (坎) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111010`

## Judgment
Waiting with sincerity brings light and success. Perseverance brings good fortune; it furthers one to cross great water.

## Image
Clouds rise to heaven — the noble one eats, drinks, and is joyous.

## Interpretation
Not paralysis — active waiting. The storm is forming; nourish yourself, stay confident, your moment will come.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Waiting in the meadow — stay with what's enduring; no blame.
- **Line 2** — Waiting on the sand — small talk, ends well.
- **Line 3** — Waiting in the mud — invites the enemy. Stay alert.
- **Line 4** — Waiting in blood — get out of the pit.
- **Line 5** — Waiting at meat and drink — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Falls into the pit. Three uninvited guests — honor them, ends well.
