# 35. 晉 Jìn — Progress / Advancing

**Korean** — 화지진 (火地晉)
**Trigrams** — Fire (離) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000101`

## Judgment
Progress. The powerful prince is honored with horses in large numbers; in a single day he is granted audience three times.

## Image
The sun rises over the earth — the noble one brightens their own bright virtue.

## Interpretation
Sun cresting the horizon — rapid, visible progress. Recognition flowing in. Keep cultivating your own light, not just chasing the spotlight.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Progressing — but turned back. Perseverance brings good fortune. If one meets with no confidence, one should remain calm. No mistake.
- **Line 2** — Progress with sorrow. Perseverance brings good fortune. One receives great happiness from one's ancestress.
- **Line 3** — All are in accord — remorse disappears.
- **Line 4** — Progress like a hamster — perseverance brings danger.
- **Line 5** — Remorse disappears. Take not gain and loss to heart. Undertakings bring good fortune. Everything serves to further.
- **Line 6** — Progressing with the horns is permissible only to chastise one's own city. To be conscious of danger brings good fortune. No blame. Perseverance brings humiliation.
