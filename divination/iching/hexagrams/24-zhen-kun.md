# 24. 復 Fù — Return / Returning

**Korean** — 지뢰복 (地雷復)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Thunder (震)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `100000`

## Judgment
Return. Success. Going out and coming in without error. Friends come without blame. On the seventh day comes return. It furthers one to have somewhere to go.

## Image
Thunder within the earth — kings of old closed the passes at the time of solstice, merchants and strangers did not travel.

## Interpretation
Light returning after the longest night. The first sprout. Welcome the small new beginning gently; don't force it.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Return from a short distance — no need for remorse. Great good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Quiet return — good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Repeated return — danger, no blame.
- **Line 4** — Walking in the midst of others, one returns alone.
- **Line 5** — Noblehearted return — no remorse.
- **Line 6** — Missing the return — misfortune. Misfortune from within and without.
