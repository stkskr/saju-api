# 4. 蒙 Méng — Youthful Folly / Enveloping

**Korean** — 산수몽 (山水蒙)
**Trigrams** — Mountain (艮) over Water (坎)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `010001`

## Judgment
Youthful folly has success. The teacher does not seek the student; the student seeks the teacher.

## Image
A spring wells up at the foot of the mountain — the noble one fosters character by thorough action.

## Interpretation
Beginner energy. Don't fake what you don't know — humility is the curriculum. Ask once with full sincerity; pestering muddies the water.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Discipline the young — fetters are removed only after lessons learned.
- **Line 2** — Bearing fools with kindness brings good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Don't take a maiden who throws herself at a man. No advantage.
- **Line 4** — Entangled folly brings humiliation.
- **Line 5** — Childlike folly — good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Punishment for folly — don't be vengeful; just prevent further wrong.
