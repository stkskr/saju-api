# 12. 否 Pǐ — Standstill / Obstruction

**Korean** — 천지비 (天地否)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000111`

## Judgment
Standstill — evil people do not further the perseverance of the noble. The great departs, the small approaches.

## Image
Heaven and earth do not unite — standstill. The noble one falls back on inner worth to escape the difficulties.

## Interpretation
Opposite of Peace. Things stuck, communication broken, the wrong people in charge. Retreat into integrity, wait it out.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — When grass is pulled, it brings others — perseverance, good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Bearing and enduring — good fortune for the small, standstill for the great.
- **Line 3** — They bear shame.
- **Line 4** — Acting at the command of the highest — no blame, those of like mind partake.
- **Line 5** — Standstill gives way. Good fortune for the great person. 'What if it should fail?' — tied to mulberry shoots.
- **Line 6** — The standstill comes to an end. First standstill, then good fortune.
