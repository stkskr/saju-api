# 33. 遯 Dùn — Retreat / Withdrawing

**Korean** — 천산돈 (天山遯)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Mountain (艮)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `001111`

## Judgment
Retreat. Success. In what is small, perseverance furthers.

## Image
Mountain under heaven — the noble one keeps the inferior person at a distance, not angrily but with reserve.

## Interpretation
Strategic exit. Not running away — choosing your battles. When dark forces rise, withdraw with grace and resources intact.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — At the tail in retreat — danger. One must not wish to undertake anything.
- **Line 2** — He holds him fast with yellow oxhide — no one can tear him loose.
- **Line 3** — A halted retreat is nerve-wracking and dangerous. To keep people as men- and maidservants brings good fortune.
- **Line 4** — Voluntary retreat brings good fortune to the noble one and downfall to the inferior person.
- **Line 5** — Friendly retreat — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 6** — Cheerful retreat. Everything serves to further.
