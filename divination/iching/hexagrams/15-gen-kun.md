# 15. 謙 Qiān — Modesty / Humility

**Korean** — 지산겸 (地山謙)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Mountain (艮)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `001000`

## Judgment
Modesty creates success. The noble one carries things through.

## Image
Within the earth, a mountain — the noble one reduces what is too much and augments what is too little.

## Interpretation
The mountain bowing low. Real power that doesn't need announcement. The only hexagram where all six lines are favorable — modesty is just that good.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — A noble one modest about their modesty — crosses great water, good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Modesty that comes to expression — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 3** — A noble one of merit who is modest — completes their work, good fortune.
- **Line 4** — Nothing that does not further by stirring up modesty.
- **Line 5** — No boasting of wealth before one's neighbor — useful to attack with force.
- **Line 6** — Modesty that comes to expression — useful to set armies in motion to chastise one's own city.
