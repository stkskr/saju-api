# 7. 師 Shī — The Army / Leading

**Korean** — 지수사 (地水師)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Water (坎)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `010000`

## Judgment
The army requires perseverance and a strong leader. Good fortune without blame.

## Image
Water within earth — the noble one increases their followers by tolerance toward the people.

## Interpretation
Mobilizing a group toward a hard goal. Discipline and clarity of command. Lead from purpose, not ego.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Army must set out in proper order. Without order, misfortune.
- **Line 2** — In the midst of the army — good fortune, the king grants triple honors.
- **Line 3** — Army carries corpses — misfortune.
- **Line 4** — Army retreats — no blame.
- **Line 5** — Game in the field — take it, no blame. Old leader leads, young leader carries corpses.
- **Line 6** — Great prince issues commands, founding states. Don't use inferior people.
