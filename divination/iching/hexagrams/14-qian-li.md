# 14. 大有 Dà Yǒu — Great Possession / Great Holding

**Korean** — 화천대유 (火天大有)
**Trigrams** — Fire (離) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111101`

## Judgment
Great possession — supreme success.

## Image
Fire in heaven above — the noble one curbs evil and furthers good, obeying the benevolent will of heaven.

## Interpretation
You've got resources — material, social, creative. The art is stewardship without ego. Share, refine, don't hoard.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — No relations with what is harmful — no blame. Recognize hardship and there will be no blame.
- **Line 2** — Big wagon to load — undertakings, no blame.
- **Line 3** — A prince offers it to the Son of Heaven — small people can't do this.
- **Line 4** — He distinguishes himself by not showing off — no blame.
- **Line 5** — His truthfulness inspires sincerity in others — dignified bearing, good fortune.
- **Line 6** — He is blessed from heaven. Good fortune, nothing unfavorable.
