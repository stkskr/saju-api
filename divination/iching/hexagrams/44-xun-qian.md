# 44. 姤 Gòu — Coming to Meet / Encountering

**Korean** — 천풍구 (天風姤)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Wind (巽)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `011111`

## Judgment
Coming to meet. The maiden is powerful. One should not marry such a maiden.

## Image
Under heaven, wind — the prince acts in dispatching their commands and proclaims them to the four quarters of the heavens.

## Interpretation
A small dark thing has entered. Subtle, attractive, dangerous if unchecked. Catch the pattern early — don't 'marry' the problem.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — It must be checked with a brake of bronze. Perseverance brings good fortune. If one lets it take its course, one experiences misfortune. Even a lean pig has it in itself to rage around.
- **Line 2** — There is a fish in the tank. No blame. Does not further guests.
- **Line 3** — There is no skin on his thighs and walking comes hard. Awareness of danger — no great blame.
- **Line 4** — No fish in the tank. This leads to misfortune.
- **Line 5** — A melon covered with willow leaves. Hidden lines. Then it drops down from heaven.
- **Line 6** — He comes to meet with his horns. Humiliation. No blame.
