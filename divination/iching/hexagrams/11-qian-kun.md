# 11. 泰 Tài — Peace / Pervading

**Korean** — 지천태 (地天泰)
**Trigrams** — Earth (坤) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111000`

## Judgment
Peace — the small departs, the great approaches. Good fortune, success.

## Image
Heaven and earth unite — peace. The ruler divides and completes the course of heaven and earth.

## Interpretation
The good times. Yin descends, yang rises, they meet — natural harmony. Enjoy it but don't get sloppy; even peace has a shelf life.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Pulling up ribbon grass takes others along — undertakings bring good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Bearing with the uncultured, crossing the river — supreme good fortune.
- **Line 3** — No plain not followed by a slope; no going without return. Sincerity brings blessing.
- **Line 4** — Fluttering down without boasting of wealth — sincerity unfeigned.
- **Line 5** — The sovereign gives his daughter in marriage — supreme good fortune.
- **Line 6** — The wall falls back into the moat. Don't fight — accept the turn.
