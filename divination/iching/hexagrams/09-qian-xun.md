# 9. 小畜 Xiǎo Xù — Small Taming / Small Accumulation

**Korean** — 풍천소축 (風天小畜)
**Trigrams** — Wind (巽) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111011`

## Judgment
Small taming has success. Dense clouds, no rain from our western region.

## Image
Wind drives across heaven — the noble one refines the outward aspect of their nature.

## Interpretation
Gentle but persistent restraint. Not the time for big moves; the time for incremental discipline. Clouds gathering — rain is coming, not yet.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Return to the way — how could there be blame? Good fortune.
- **Line 2** — He allows himself to be drawn into returning — good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Spokes burst from the wagon — husband and wife roll their eyes.
- **Line 4** — If you are sincere, blood vanishes and fear gives way. No blame.
- **Line 5** — Sincere and loyally attached — rich in his neighbor's company.
- **Line 6** — Rain comes, rest comes. Danger if the wife persists in pressing on.
