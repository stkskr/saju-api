# 23. 剝 Bō — Splitting Apart / Stripping

**Korean** — 산지박 (山地剝)
**Trigrams** — Mountain (艮) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000001`

## Judgment
Splitting apart. It does not further one to go anywhere.

## Image
The mountain rests on the earth — those above can ensure their position only by giving generously to those below.

## Interpretation
Things falling apart. Don't fight the dissolution; protect the seed for what comes next. Misfortune now sets up renewal later.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — The leg of the bed is split — those who persevere are destroyed. Misfortune.
- **Line 2** — The bed is split at the edge — those who persevere are destroyed. Misfortune.
- **Line 3** — He splits with them — no blame.
- **Line 4** — The bed is split up to the skin — misfortune.
- **Line 5** — A shoal of fishes. Favor comes through the court ladies. Everything acts to further.
- **Line 6** — There is a large fruit still uneaten. The noble one receives a carriage. The house of the inferior is split apart.
