# 37. 家人 Jiā Rén — The Family / People in the Home

**Korean** — 풍화가인 (風火家人)
**Trigrams** — Wind (巽) over Fire (離)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `101011`

## Judgment
The family — the perseverance of the woman furthers.

## Image
Wind comes forth from fire — the noble one has substance in their words and duration in their way of life.

## Interpretation
The household as microcosm. Healthy family = clear roles, real warmth, sturdy walls. The wisdom learned at home governs everywhere.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Firm seclusion within the family — remorse disappears.
- **Line 2** — She should not follow her whims. She must attend within to the food. Perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 3** — When tempers flare up in the family, too great severity brings remorse. Good fortune nonetheless. When woman and child dally and laugh, it leads in the end to humiliation.
- **Line 4** — She is the treasure of the house — great good fortune.
- **Line 5** — As a king he approaches his family. Fear not. Good fortune.
- **Line 6** — His work commands respect — in the end good fortune comes.
