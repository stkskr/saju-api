# 20. 觀 Guān — Contemplation / Viewing

**Korean** — 풍지관 (風地觀)
**Trigrams** — Wind (巽) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000011`

## Judgment
Contemplation. The ablution has been made, but not yet the offering. Full of trust they look up to him.

## Image
Wind blows over the earth — kings of old visited the regions of the world, contemplated the people, and gave them instruction.

## Interpretation
Stepping back to really see. What's actually going on here? Modeling clarity rather than reacting. Watch and be watched.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Boylike contemplation — for an inferior person no blame, for a noble one humiliation.
- **Line 2** — Contemplation through a crack of the door — furthering for the perseverance of a woman.
- **Line 3** — Contemplation of my life decides whether to advance or retreat.
- **Line 4** — Contemplation of the light of the kingdom — furthering to exert influence as the guest of a king.
- **Line 5** — Contemplation of my life — the noble one is without blame.
- **Line 6** — Contemplation of his life — the noble one is without blame.
