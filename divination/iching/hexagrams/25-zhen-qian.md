# 25. 無妄 Wú Wàng — Innocence / Without Pretense

**Korean** — 천뢰무망 (天雷無妄)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Thunder (震)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `100111`

## Judgment
Innocence — supreme success. Perseverance furthers. If one is not as they should be, they have misfortune.

## Image
Under heaven, thunder rolls — all things attain the natural state of innocence. The kings of old fostered creatures with abundance, in harmony with time.

## Interpretation
Acting from your unconditioned nature, no scheming. Things go right when you stop trying to make them go right. Surrender the agenda.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Innocent behavior brings good fortune.
- **Line 2** — If one does not count on the harvest while plowing, nor on the use of the ground while clearing — undertakings furthering.
- **Line 3** — Undeserved misfortune. The cow that was tethered by someone is the wanderer's gain, the citizen's loss.
- **Line 4** — He who can be persevering remains without blame.
- **Line 5** — Use no medicine in an illness incurred through no fault of your own — it passes of itself.
- **Line 6** — Innocent action brings misfortune. Nothing furthers.
