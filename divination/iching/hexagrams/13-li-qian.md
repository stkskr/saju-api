# 13. 同人 Tóng Rén — Fellowship with People

**Korean** — 천화동인 (天火同人)
**Trigrams** — Heaven (乾) over Fire (離)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `101111`

## Judgment
Fellowship with people in the open. Success. Furthering to cross great water; furthering perseverance of the noble.

## Image
Heaven together with fire — the noble one organizes the clans and differentiates things.

## Interpretation
Real community, not cliques. Common vision held in the open. Build alliances based on values, not convenience.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Fellowship at the gate — no blame.
- **Line 2** — Fellowship in the clan — humiliation.
- **Line 3** — Hides weapons in the thicket, climbs the high hill — three years no rising.
- **Line 4** — Climbs up on the wall — cannot attack. Good fortune.
- **Line 5** — Fellowship — first weep and lament, then laugh. Great armies meet.
- **Line 6** — Fellowship in the meadow — no remorse.
