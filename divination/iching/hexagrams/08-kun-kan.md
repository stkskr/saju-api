# 8. 比 Bǐ — Holding Together / Union

**Korean** — 수지비 (水地比)
**Trigrams** — Water (坎) over Earth (坤)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `000010`

## Judgment
Holding together brings good fortune. Inquire if your virtue is sublime and constant — then no blame. Those who hesitate come too late.

## Image
Water over earth — kings of antiquity bestowed the different states as fiefs and cultivated friendly relations.

## Interpretation
Magnetic pull toward connection. Find your people. But check your own foundation first — you can't lead bonded community from a wobbly center.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Hold to him with truth and loyalty — unexpected good fortune.
- **Line 2** — Hold together inwardly — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 3** — Hold together with the wrong people.
- **Line 4** — Hold together outwardly with him — perseverance brings good fortune.
- **Line 5** — Manifestation of holding together — the king lets the game flee.
- **Line 6** — Hold together without a head — misfortune.
