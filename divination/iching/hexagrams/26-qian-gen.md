# 26. 大畜 Dà Xù — Great Taming / Great Accumulation

**Korean** — 산천대축 (山天大畜)
**Trigrams** — Mountain (艮) over Heaven (乾)
**Binary (bottom→top)** — `111001`

## Judgment
Great taming. Perseverance furthers. Not eating at home brings good fortune. Furthering to cross great water.

## Image
Heaven within the mountain — the noble one acquaintances themselves with many sayings of antiquity and many deeds of the past in order to strengthen their character thereby.

## Interpretation
Big energy held back to build up power. Like a bow being drawn. Study, train, accumulate — the release will be epic when it comes.

## Changing line notes
- **Line 1** — Danger is at hand — beneficial to desist.
- **Line 2** — The axle-trees are taken from the wagon.
- **Line 3** — A good horse that follows others — awareness of danger and perseverance furthers. Daily practice in chariot driving and armed defense.
- **Line 4** — Headboard of a young bull — great good fortune.
- **Line 5** — Tusk of a gelded boar — good fortune.
- **Line 6** — He attains the way of heaven — success.
