# Empty branches (공망/空亡) — lookup

The 60-jiazi cycle is divided into six 旬 (순) of 10 pillars each. Each 순 starts with a 甲 stem and ends with a 癸 stem — leaving 2 of the 12 branches without a stem partner. Those 2 are the 'empty' branches for every pillar in that 순.

| 旬 | Pillars (range) | Empty branches | Korean label |
|----|------------------|----------------|--------------|
| 갑자순 | 甲子 → 癸酉 (#1–10) | 戌, 亥 | 갑자순 (甲子旬) — 戌, 亥 공망 |
| 갑술순 | 甲戌 → 癸未 (#11–20) | 申, 酉 | 갑술순 (甲戌旬) — 申, 酉 공망 |
| 갑신순 | 甲申 → 癸巳 (#21–30) | 午, 未 | 갑신순 (甲申旬) — 午, 未 공망 |
| 갑오순 | 甲午 → 癸卯 (#31–40) | 辰, 巳 | 갑오순 (甲午旬) — 辰, 巳 공망 |
| 갑진순 | 甲辰 → 癸丑 (#41–50) | 寅, 卯 | 갑진순 (甲辰旬) — 寅, 卯 공망 |
| 갑인순 | 甲寅 → 癸亥 (#51–60) | 子, 丑 | 갑인순 (甲寅旬) — 子, 丑 공망 |

## How to determine empty branches in a chart

1. Find the Day Pillar (일주) in the 60-jiazi cycle.
2. Identify which 旬 (group of 10) it belongs to.
3. The two empty branches for that 旬 are the chart's 공망.
4. Check whether either of those branches appears in the Year, Month, or Hour pillar — if it does, that pillar's domain is read as 'empty.'

## Reading guidance

- **Year pillar 공망** — early life, ancestors, or social position feels disconnected.
- **Month pillar 공망** — career, parents, or formative environment feels hollow or unstable.
- **Hour pillar 공망** — late life, children, or legacy feels uncertain.
- 공망 is NOT automatically bad. For someone pursuing a contemplative or spiritual life, an empty pillar can support detachment from that domain.
- A 공망 branch that participates in combinations or trinities can be 'reactivated.'
