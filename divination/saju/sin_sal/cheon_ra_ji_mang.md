# 天羅地網 (천라지망 cheon_ra_ji_mang)

**Category** — inauspicious

**Branches involved** — 戌, 亥, 辰, 巳

## Interpretation

Heaven Net / Earth Net. 戌亥 = Heaven Net (for fire/yang day masters), 辰巳 = Earth Net (for water/yin day masters). Restriction, confinement, hard to escape circumstances. Can manifest as imprisonment (literal or figurative), prolonged illness, or being stuck.
