# 文曲貴人 (문곡귀인 mun_gok_gwi_in)

**Category** — auspicious

**Lookup by** — day_stem

## Interpretation

Literary Bent star — sister to 문창귀인 but emphasizes artistic, lyrical, expressive talent (poetry, music, narrative) rather than scholarly. Located at the day stem's death (死) position, which inverts the meaning into deep aesthetic sensitivity.
