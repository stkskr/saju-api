# 天殺 (천살 cheon_sal)

**Category** — inauspicious

## Interpretation

Heaven Killer. One of the 'twelve killers' (십이신살). Sudden, externally caused setbacks — natural disasters, things outside your control. The branch four positions after the seasonal trinity.
