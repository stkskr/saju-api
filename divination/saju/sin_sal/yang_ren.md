# 羊刃 (양인 yang-in) — Sheep Blade / Edge

**Type** — intense (often inauspicious)
**Category** — power / aggression
**Calculated from** — Day Stem

## Interpretation
Star of sharp, sometimes violent power. Strength that can wound oneself or others. Surgeons, soldiers, athletes can channel it well; impulsive harm can come if uncontrolled.
