# 天醫星 (천의성 cheon_ui_seong)

**Category** — auspicious

**Lookup by** — month_branch (previous branch)

## Interpretation

Heavenly Doctor star — the branch one position before the month branch. Indicates affinity for healing, medicine, psychology, counseling, or restorative work. Sensitivity to others' suffering.
