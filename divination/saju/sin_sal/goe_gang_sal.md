# 魁罡殺 (괴강살 goe_gang_sal)

**Category** — mixed

**Pillars** — 庚辰, 庚戌, 壬辰, 壬戌, 戊戌, 戊辰

## Interpretation

Kuigang — leadership of the Big Dipper. Extraordinary force, intelligence, command. Either greatness or great trouble — rarely middle ground. In women, classical texts considered it harsh; modern readings reframe as exceptional independence and authority.
