# 鬼門關殺 (귀문관살 gwi_mun_gwan_sal)

**Category** — inauspicious

## Pairs

- 子 ↔ 酉
- 丑 ↔ 午
- 寅 ↔ 未
- 卯 ↔ 申
- 辰 ↔ 亥
- 巳 ↔ 戌

## Interpretation

Ghost Gate. Indicates psychological sensitivity, vivid dreams, susceptibility to obsession or unusual mental states. Can also mean strong intuition or affinity for spiritual work.
