# 白虎殺 (백호살 baek_ho_sal)

**Category** — inauspicious

**Pillars** — 甲辰, 乙未, 丙戌, 丁丑, 戊辰, 壬戌, 癸丑

## Interpretation

White Tiger killer. Specific pillars associated with bloodshed, sudden accidents, violent death (classically), or in modern reading: sharp, abrupt life events, surgeries, separations. Powerful but dangerous energy.
