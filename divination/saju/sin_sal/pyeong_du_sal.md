# 平頭殺 (평두살 pyeong_du_sal)

**Category** — mixed

**Stems/branches involved** — 甲, 丙, 丁, 壬, 子, 辰

## Interpretation

Flat-Head killer. Characters whose written form has a flat top stroke. Classically associated with monastics, ascetics, religious or spiritual paths — also with hair loss or head injuries. Modern: spiritual or contemplative inclination.
