# 亡神 (망신 mang-sin) — Disgrace / Loss-of-Self Star

**Type** — inauspicious
**Category** — exposure / scandal
**Calculated from** — Year or Day Branch

## Interpretation
Star of public exposure, embarrassment, loss of face. Used well, brings hard-won self-knowledge; used badly, scandals and reputational hits.
