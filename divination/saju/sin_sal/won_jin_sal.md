# 怨嗔殺 (원진살 won_jin_sal)

**Category** — inauspicious

## Pairs

- 子 ↔ 未
- 丑 ↔ 午
- 寅 ↔ 酉
- 卯 ↔ 申
- 辰 ↔ 亥
- 巳 ↔ 戌

## Interpretation

Resentment killer. Six specific branch pairs that breed silent grudges — relationships that look fine on the surface but accumulate hidden bitterness. Often shows up between in-laws, siblings, or business partners.
