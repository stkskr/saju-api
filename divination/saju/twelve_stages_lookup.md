## How to read this

Find your Day Master (day stem). Then look at each of the four branch positions (year, month, day, hour). The stage shown tells you how vital your day master's energy is in that pillar.

- **장생/長生** — birth — fresh start, new energy
- **목욕/沐浴** — bathing — vulnerability, indulgence, exposure
- **관대/冠帶** — cap and sash — coming of age, putting on the suit
- **임관/臨官** — taking office — ready to perform, peer/colleague support
- **제왕/帝旺** — emperor peak — maximum strength, also pride
- **쇠/衰** — decline — past peak, time to consolidate
- **병/病** — illness — vulnerability, slowdown
- **사/死** — death — exhausted force
- **묘/墓** — tomb / storage — collected, hidden, dormant
- **절/絕** — cut off — total disconnect from element
- **태/胎** — conception — re-forming, potential reborn
- **양/養** — nurture — gestation, being raised

## Reading strength

When the Month Branch (most important for assessing day master strength) places the day stem at 장생, 임관, or 제왕, the day master is strong. At 쇠, 병, 사, 묘, 절, the day master is weak.
