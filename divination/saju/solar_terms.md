# Twenty-four solar terms (이십사절기)

Saju months change at these solar boundaries, NOT at the lunar new month.

The 12 odd-numbered terms (節) are the month-start markers; the 12 even ones (氣) fall mid-month.

## 1. 立春 (입춘 ip-chun) — Beginning of Spring
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Feb 3–5
- **Branch month** — 寅
- Start of the Saju astrological year — the Year Pillar changes here, NOT at Lunar New Year.

## 2. 雨水 (우수 u-su) — Rain Water
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Feb 18–20
- **Branch month** — 寅 (mid)
- Snow melts; rains begin. Mid-寅 month.

## 3. 驚蟄 (경칩 gyeong-chip) — Awakening of Insects
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Mar 5–7
- **Branch month** — 卯
- Thunder wakes hibernating creatures. Start of 卯 month.

## 4. 春分 (춘분 chun-bun) — Spring Equinox
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Mar 20–22
- **Branch month** — 卯 (mid)
- Day equals night. Mid-卯 month.

## 5. 清明 (청명 cheong-myeong) — Pure Brightness
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Apr 4–6
- **Branch month** — 辰
- Clear, bright spring. Start of 辰 month. Tomb-sweeping festival.

## 6. 穀雨 (곡우 gok-u) — Grain Rain
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Apr 19–21
- **Branch month** — 辰 (mid)
- Rain feeds the grain. Mid-辰 month.

## 7. 立夏 (입하 ip-ha) — Beginning of Summer
- **Approx Gregorian date** — May 5–7
- **Branch month** — 巳
- Start of 巳 month.

## 8. 小滿 (소만 so-man) — Grain Buds
- **Approx Gregorian date** — May 20–22
- **Branch month** — 巳 (mid)
- Grain plumps but not yet ripe. Mid-巳 month.

## 9. 芒種 (망종 mang-jong) — Grain in Ear
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jun 5–7
- **Branch month** — 午
- Awns form on grains. Start of 午 month.

## 10. 夏至 (하지 ha-ji) — Summer Solstice
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jun 21–22
- **Branch month** — 午 (mid)
- Longest day. Mid-午 month. Peak yang.

## 11. 小暑 (소서 so-seo) — Minor Heat
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jul 6–8
- **Branch month** — 未
- Hot but not peak. Start of 未 month.

## 12. 大暑 (대서 dae-seo) — Major Heat
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jul 22–24
- **Branch month** — 未 (mid)
- Hottest period. Mid-未 month.

## 13. 立秋 (입추 ip-chu) — Beginning of Autumn
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Aug 7–9
- **Branch month** — 申
- Start of 申 month — autumn officially begins.

## 14. 處暑 (처서 cheo-seo) — End of Heat
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Aug 22–24
- **Branch month** — 申 (mid)
- Heat retreats. Mid-申 month.

## 15. 白露 (백로 baek-ro) — White Dew
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Sep 7–9
- **Branch month** — 酉
- Cool nights bring dew. Start of 酉 month.

## 16. 秋分 (추분 chu-bun) — Autumn Equinox
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Sep 22–24
- **Branch month** — 酉 (mid)
- Day equals night. Mid-酉 month.

## 17. 寒露 (한로 han-ro) — Cold Dew
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Oct 7–9
- **Branch month** — 戌
- Dew turns colder. Start of 戌 month.

## 18. 霜降 (상강 sang-gang) — Frost Descends
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Oct 23–24
- **Branch month** — 戌 (mid)
- First frost. Mid-戌 month.

## 19. 立冬 (입동 ip-dong) — Beginning of Winter
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Nov 7–8
- **Branch month** — 亥
- Start of 亥 month — winter begins.

## 20. 小雪 (소설 so-seol) — Minor Snow
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Nov 22–23
- **Branch month** — 亥 (mid)
- Light snow possible. Mid-亥 month.

## 21. 大雪 (대설 dae-seol) — Major Snow
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Dec 6–8
- **Branch month** — 子
- Heavy snow. Start of 子 month.

## 22. 冬至 (동지 dong-ji) — Winter Solstice
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Dec 21–23
- **Branch month** — 子 (mid)
- Longest night, but yang seed is born. Mid-子 month.

## 23. 小寒 (소한 so-han) — Minor Cold
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jan 5–7
- **Branch month** — 丑
- Cold deepens. Start of 丑 month.

## 24. 大寒 (대한 dae-han) — Major Cold
- **Approx Gregorian date** — Jan 20–21
- **Branch month** — 丑 (mid)
- Coldest period. Mid-丑 month. Year ends with the next term, 立春.
