# Five elements (오행)

## 木 wood (목 mok)

- **Season** — spring
- **Direction** — east
- **Color** — green/blue
- **Organs** — yin: liver, yang: gallbladder
- **Generates** — fire
- **Controls** — earth
- **Generated by** — water
- **Controlled by** — metal
- **Stems** — 甲, 乙
- **Branches** — 寅, 卯
- **Positive emotion** — kindness, vision
- **Negative emotion** — anger, frustration

Growth-oriented, future-focused, planner. Wants to expand and create. Frustration when blocked.

## 火 fire (화 hwa)

- **Season** — summer
- **Direction** — south
- **Color** — red
- **Organs** — yin: heart, yang: small intestine
- **Generates** — earth
- **Controls** — metal
- **Generated by** — wood
- **Controlled by** — water
- **Stems** — 丙, 丁
- **Branches** — 巳, 午
- **Positive emotion** — joy, passion
- **Negative emotion** — anxiety, mania

Expressive, charismatic, present-focused. Wants connection and visibility. Burnout when unchecked.

## 土 earth (토 to)

- **Season** — late summer / transitions
- **Direction** — center
- **Color** — yellow/brown
- **Organs** — yin: spleen, yang: stomach
- **Generates** — metal
- **Controls** — water
- **Generated by** — fire
- **Controlled by** — wood
- **Stems** — 戊, 己
- **Branches** — 辰, 未, 戌, 丑
- **Positive emotion** — trust, care
- **Negative emotion** — worry, overthinking

Stabilizing, caring, present. Wants security and belonging. Stagnates when stuck in worry.

## 金 metal (금 geum)

- **Season** — autumn
- **Direction** — west
- **Color** — white/silver
- **Organs** — yin: lung, yang: large intestine
- **Generates** — water
- **Controls** — wood
- **Generated by** — earth
- **Controlled by** — fire
- **Stems** — 庚, 辛
- **Branches** — 申, 酉
- **Positive emotion** — courage, righteousness
- **Negative emotion** — grief, rigidity

Refined, principled, structuring. Wants order and value. Cold or grieving when imbalanced.

## 水 water (수 su)

- **Season** — winter
- **Direction** — north
- **Color** — black/dark blue
- **Organs** — yin: kidney, yang: bladder
- **Generates** — wood
- **Controls** — fire
- **Generated by** — metal
- **Controlled by** — earth
- **Stems** — 壬, 癸
- **Branches** — 亥, 子
- **Positive emotion** — wisdom, will
- **Negative emotion** — fear, paralysis

Reflective, deep, conserving. Wants understanding and quiet power. Withdraws when fearful.
