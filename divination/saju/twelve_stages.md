# Twelve life stages (십이운성)

Maps an element's life cycle through the twelve branches. Used to read vitality of a stem in a given pillar.

## 1. 長生 (장생 jang-saeng) — Long Life / Birth

**Phase** — newborn
**Keywords** — birth, fresh start, potential, innocence, support

Birth. Fresh energy entering the world. Surrounded by care, optimistic, supported.

## 2. 沐浴 (목욕 mok-yok) — Bathing / Cleansing

**Phase** — infant bathing
**Keywords** — vulnerability, indulgence, temptation, exposed, instability

Bathing the newborn. Naked, exposed, sensual. Can indicate vulnerability, indulgence, scandal energy, or restlessness.

## 3. 冠帶 (관대 gwan-dae) — Coming of Age / Capping

**Phase** — adolescence
**Keywords** — ambition, self-assertion, uniform, pride, initiation

Putting on the official cap and belt. Coming-of-age ambition, self-assertion, pride. Eager to prove.

## 4. 建祿 (건록 geon-rok) — Establishing Salary / Prosperity

**Phase** — young adult professional
**Keywords** — stability, career foundation, competence, independence, reward

Established in office, earning a salary. Independent, competent, building career foundation.

## 5. 帝旺 (제왕 je-wang) — Emperor's Peak / Maximum Power

**Phase** — peak of life
**Keywords** — peak, authority, fullness, command, danger of decline

Sovereign at peak. Maximum strength of the element. Authority, presence — but also the moment just before decline begins.

## 6. 衰 (쇠 soe) — Decline

**Phase** — first weakening
**Keywords** — fatigue, first wane, wisdom of decline, softening, maturity

First weakening after the peak. Mature, slightly tired, more wisdom and less force.

## 7. 病 (병 byeong) — Illness

**Phase** — sickness
**Keywords** — vulnerability, sensitivity, compassion, introspection, weakness

Sickbed phase. Sensitive, introspective, compassionate. The element is weakened; reflection takes the place of action.

## 8. 死 (사 sa) — Death

**Phase** — stillness
**Keywords** — stillness, philosophical, withdrawal, no force, depth

Death. The element has no outer force here. Quiet, philosophical, withdrawn — but capable of deep work inwardly.

## 9. 墓 (묘 myo) — Tomb / Storage

**Phase** — burial / storehouse
**Keywords** — storage, preservation, memory, secrecy, inheritance

The element stored in the tomb / storehouse. Hidden, preserved, available later. Often a wealth-storing position.

## 10. 絕 (절 jeol) — Extinction / Severance

**Phase** — between worlds
**Keywords** — void, letting go, transition, endings, rebirth potential

Cut off completely. Between worlds. Endings without immediate new beginnings. But the seed of return is here.

## 11. 胎 (태 tae) — Embryo / Conception

**Phase** — conception
**Keywords** — new conception, hidden start, fragile, potential, gestation

Conceived in the womb. New cycle begun in hidden form. Fragile, full of pure potential.

## 12. 養 (양 yang) — Nurturance / Womb

**Phase** — gestation
**Keywords** — growth in safety, nourishment, preparation, patience, almost born

Nourished in the womb. Growing safely, almost ready to enter the world. Patient final preparation.
