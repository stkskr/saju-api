# Heavenly stem five combinations (천간오합 / 天干五合)

Five paired stems that combine. When the surrounding chart supports it (right season, sufficient root), they can *transform* into a new element (合化).

| Stem A | Stem B | Korean | Transforms to | Name |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 甲 | 己 | 갑기 | earth (土) | — |
| 乙 | 庚 | 을경 | metal (金) | — |
| 丙 | 辛 | 병신 | water (水) | — |
| 丁 | 壬 | 정임 | wood (木) | — |
| 戊 | 癸 | 무계 | fire (火) | — |

## Interpretations

### 甲 + 己 (갑기)
甲己合化土 — Yang Wood and Yin Earth combine into Earth. The Combination of Centrality and Loyalty (中正之合). Implies trustworthiness, principled center.

### 乙 + 庚 (을경)
乙庚合化金 — Yin Wood and Yang Metal combine into Metal. The Combination of Benevolence and Righteousness (仁義之合). Implies fairness, courage, willingness to do hard things.

### 丙 + 辛 (병신)
丙辛合化水 — Yang Fire and Yin Metal combine into Water. The Combination of Authority (威制之合). Power dynamics; can produce charisma but also coercion.

### 丁 + 壬 (정임)
丁壬合化木 — Yin Fire and Yang Water combine into Wood. The Combination of Sensuality (淫慝之合). Passionate, creative, sometimes overly attached or emotional.

### 戊 + 癸 (무계)
戊癸合化火 — Yang Earth and Yin Water combine into Fire. The Combination of Beauty (無情之合). Mismatched ages classically; aesthetic, sometimes detached.

## Reading notes

- Combination always implies *bonding* even when full transformation doesn't occur.
- Day Master combining with a Ten God can change how that god behaves.
- Combination of the Day Master 'binds' the day master toward the partner — sometimes loss of independence.
