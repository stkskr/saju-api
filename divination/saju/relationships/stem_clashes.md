# Heavenly stem clashes (천간충 / 天干相沖)

Stems seven positions apart in the cycle clash. Yang clashes yang, yin clashes yin. Earth stems (戊, 己) are central and have no clash partner.

| Stem A | Stem B | Dynamic |
|---|---|---|
| 甲 | 庚 | wood vs metal — metal cuts wood |
| 乙 | 辛 | yin wood vs yin metal — pruning shears |
| 丙 | 壬 | fire vs water — water extinguishes fire |
| 丁 | 癸 | yin fire vs yin water — small flame, mist |

Clashes between stems are generally less violent than between branches but still disturbing — the clashing element on top of the chart creates visible friction in personality or external life.
