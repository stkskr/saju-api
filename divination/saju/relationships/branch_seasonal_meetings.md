# Seasonal three-meetings (방합 / 三會)

Four branch triplets formed by three consecutive same-season branches. Even stronger than 삼합 because all three branches share the same elemental nature.

| Meeting | Korean | Element / Direction | Reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| 寅 卯 辰 | 인묘진 | wood (spring/east) | Spring directional combo — the eastern wood quarter. |
| 巳 午 未 | 사오미 | fire (summer/south) | Summer directional combo — the southern fire quarter. |
| 申 酉 戌 | 신유술 | metal (autumn/west) | Autumn directional combo — the western metal quarter. |
| 亥 子 丑 | 해자축 | water (winter/north) | Winter directional combo — the northern water quarter. |

When a full seasonal meeting appears in a chart, that element/direction becomes dominant — often a defining feature of the personality and life path.
