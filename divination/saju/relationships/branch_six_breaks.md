# Branch six-breaks (육파 / 六破)

Six pairs that disrupt or undermine. Weaker than clashes but creates instability in whatever the affected pillars represent.

| Branch A | Branch B | Korean | Reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| 子 | 酉 | 자유 | Disruption to plans, broken connections. |
| 丑 | 辰 | 축진 | Earth fragmentation — broken foundations. |
| 寅 | 亥 | 인해 | Note: also a six-combination — break weakens but doesn't destroy. |
| 卯 | 午 | 묘오 | Wood-fire break — burnout, exhausted growth. |
| 巳 | 申 | 사신 | Note: also a six-combination and a punishment — three relationships at once. |
| 未 | 戌 | 미술 | Dry earth meeting dry earth — friction without nourishment. |

Note that 寅亥 and 巳申 appear in multiple relationship tables — they're simultaneously combinations *and* breaks, indicating built-in instability within bonding.
