# Branch six-harms (육해 / 六害)

Six pairs that create quiet, often hidden friction. Less dramatic than clashes — more like slow erosion or unspoken resentment.

| Branch A | Branch B | Korean | Reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| 子 | 未 | 자미 | Hidden enmity — small frictions, betrayals close to home. |
| 丑 | 午 | 축오 | Mismatched timing — secret resentments. |
| 寅 | 巳 | 인사 | Ambition friction — both want to lead; also a punishment pair. |
| 卯 | 辰 | 묘진 | Quiet sabotage — wood depleting fragile earth. |
| 申 | 亥 | 신해 | Mutual undermining — metal contaminating water. |
| 酉 | 戌 | 유술 | Familial harms — metal and dry earth in tension. |

Harms often appear in relationship dynamics — between spouses, siblings, business partners — as the kind of friction that builds up without an obvious blowup.
