# Three harmonies (삼합 / 三合)

Four branch triplets that form powerful element trinities. Each trinity contains a birth (長生), peak (帝旺), and storage (墓) of a single element.

| Trinity | Korean | Element | Reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| 申 子 辰 | 신자진 | water | Water trinity — birth (申), peak (子), storage (辰). |
| 亥 卯 未 | 해묘미 | wood | Wood trinity — birth (亥), peak (卯), storage (未). |
| 寅 午 戌 | 인오술 | fire | Fire trinity — birth (寅), peak (午), storage (戌). |
| 巳 酉 丑 | 사유축 | metal | Metal trinity — birth (巳), peak (酉), storage (丑). |

## Reading notes

- Full trinity in a chart is rare and powerful — strongly amplifies that element.
- Two of the three (반합 / half-combination) is more common and still produces a noticeable pull toward the element, especially if the *peak* (中支) is one of the two present.
- The peak branches (子, 卯, 午, 酉) are the cardinal points — most powerful in any trinity.
