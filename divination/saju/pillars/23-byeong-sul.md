**Branch** — 戌 술 (earth, dog)

## Character

Sun setting behind mountain — warm afterglow. Wise, mature beyond years, sometimes carries old sorrow.

## Day Master reading

The fire day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.
