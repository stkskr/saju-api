**Branch** — 亥 해 (water, pig)

## Character

Jewel in flowing water — washed clean, lustrous. Intelligent, refined, often gifted in writing or speech.

## Day Master reading

The metal day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (亥) is the spouse palace.
