# 乙未 (을미) — Pillar 32 of 60

**Stem** — 乙 을 (wood, yin)
**Branch** — 未 미 (earth, sheep)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 己, 丁, 乙
**Twelve-stage position** — 養 양
**Nayin** — 沙中金 사중금 (Sand-Buried Metal — metal)
**Empty branches** — 辰, 巳
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Tender vine in summer earth — wilting unless tended. Sensitive, artistic, often emotionally complex.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (未) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 辰, 巳.
