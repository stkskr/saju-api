# 丁亥 (정해) — Pillar 24 of 60

**Stem** — 丁 정 (fire, yin)
**Branch** — 亥 해 (water, pig)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 壬, 甲
**Twelve-stage position** — 胎 태
**Nayin** — 屋上土 옥상토 (Rooftop Earth — earth)
**Empty branches** — 午, 未
**Stem-on-branch** — Is controlled by (branch controls stem — pressure)

## Character

Candle on flowing water — gentle light moving through change. Intuitive, philosophical, often spiritual.

## Day Master reading

The fire day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (earth) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (亥) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 午, 未.
