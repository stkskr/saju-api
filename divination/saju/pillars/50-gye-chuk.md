# 癸丑 (계축) — Pillar 50 of 60

**Stem** — 癸 계 (water, yin)
**Branch** — 丑 축 (earth, ox)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 己, 癸, 辛
**Twelve-stage position** — 冠帶 관대
**Nayin** — 桑柘木 상자목 (Mulberry Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 寅, 卯
**Stem-on-branch** — Is controlled by (branch controls stem — pressure)

## Character

Quiet rain in frozen field — slow patient nourishment. Endurance, depth, sometimes melancholy. 백호 pillar.

## Day Master reading

The water day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (丑) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 寅, 卯.
