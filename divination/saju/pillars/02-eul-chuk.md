# 乙丑 (을축) — Pillar 2 of 60

**Stem** — 乙 을 (wood, yin)
**Branch** — 丑 축 (earth, ox)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 己, 癸, 辛
**Twelve-stage position** — 衰 쇠
**Nayin** — 海中金 해중금 (Sea Metal — metal)
**Empty branches** — 戌, 亥
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Tender vine on damp cold earth. Reserved, patient, capable of slow but persistent growth. Hidden treasure in the branch.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (丑) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 戌, 亥.
