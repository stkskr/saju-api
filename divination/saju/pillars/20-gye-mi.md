# 癸未 (계미) — Pillar 20 of 60

**Stem** — 癸 계 (water, yin)
**Branch** — 未 미 (earth, sheep)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 己, 丁, 乙
**Twelve-stage position** — 墓 묘
**Nayin** — 楊柳木 양류목 (Willow Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 申, 酉
**Stem-on-branch** — Is controlled by (branch controls stem — pressure)

## Character

Quiet rain on summer earth — relief, momentary nourishment. Compassionate, thoughtful, sometimes overlooked. Often introverted.

## Day Master reading

The water day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (未) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 申, 酉.
