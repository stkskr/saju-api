# 己卯 (기묘) — Pillar 16 of 60

**Stem** — 己 기 (earth, yin)
**Branch** — 卯 묘 (wood, rabbit)

## Character

Soft earth under spring growth — depleted by demand. Generous to a fault, can feel drained. Often artistic or in service roles.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (earth) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (卯) is the spouse palace.
