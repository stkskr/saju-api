**Branch** — 巳 사 (fire, snake)

## Character

Refined metal heated by fire — being tempered. Refined and ambitious, but at risk of internal conflict (fire melting metal).

## Day Master reading

The metal day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (巳) is the spouse palace.
