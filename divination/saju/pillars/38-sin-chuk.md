**Stem** — 辛 신 (metal, yin)
**Branch** — 丑 축 (earth, ox)

**Stem-on-branch** — Is supported (branch generates stem — nourishment)

## Character

Refined metal in frozen earth — patient storage. Disciplined, sometimes isolated, often spiritually inclined.

## Day Master reading

The metal day master is nourished by the branch (earth generates metal). Strong, well-resourced.

- This pillar's nayin element (earth) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (丑) is the spouse palace.
