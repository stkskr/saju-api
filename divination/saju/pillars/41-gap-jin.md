# 甲辰 (갑진) — Pillar 41 of 60

**Stem** — 甲 갑 (wood, yang)
**Branch** — 辰 진 (earth, dragon)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 戊, 乙, 癸
**Twelve-stage position** — 衰 쇠
**Nayin** — 覆燈火 복등화 (Covered Lamp Fire — fire)

**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Tree in fertile dragon earth — deep roots and hidden treasure. 백호 pillar (carries sharp edge). Ambitious, can be domineering.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (fire) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (辰) is the spouse palace.
