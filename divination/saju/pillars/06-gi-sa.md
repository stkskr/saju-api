# 己巳 (기사) — Pillar 6 of 60

**Stem** — 己 기 (earth, yin)
**Branch** — 巳 사 (fire, snake)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 丙, 庚, 戊
**Twelve-stage position** — 帝旺 제왕
**Nayin** — 大林木 대림목 (Great Forest Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 戌, 亥
**Stem-on-branch** — Is supported (branch generates stem — nourishment)

## Character

Soft earth warmed by fire — fertile field at noon. Nurturing, hospitable, practical. The 천을귀인 is often nearby.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master is nourished by the branch (fire generates earth). Strong, well-resourced.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (巳) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 戌, 亥.
