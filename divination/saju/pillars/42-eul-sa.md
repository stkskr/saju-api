**Stem** — 乙 을 (wood, yin)
**Branch** — 巳 사 (fire, snake)

**Nayin** — 覆燈火 복등화 (Covered Lamp Fire — fire)

**Stem-on-branch** — Leaks (stem generates branch — stem element gives energy)

## Character

Vine in summer fire — bright but at risk. Charismatic, artistic, sometimes self-consuming.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (fire) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (巳) is the spouse palace.
