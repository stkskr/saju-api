# 辛卯 (신묘) — Pillar 28 of 60

**Stem** — 辛 신 (metal, yin)
**Branch** — 卯 묘 (wood, rabbit)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 乙
**Twelve-stage position** — 絕 절
**Nayin** — 松柏木 송백목 (Pine-Cypress Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 午, 未
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Jewel meeting young growth — precision against tenderness. Refined critical mind, sharp tongue, often artistic.

## Day Master reading

The metal day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (卯) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 午, 未.
