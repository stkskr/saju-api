# 癸巳 (계사) — Pillar 30 of 60

**Stem** — 癸 계 (water, yin)
**Branch** — 巳 사 (fire, snake)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 丙, 庚, 戊
**Twelve-stage position** — 胎 태
**Nayin** — 長流水 장류수 (Long-Flowing Water — water)
**Empty branches** — 午, 未
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Quiet rain on fire — paradox, evaporation, intensity. Internally conflicted, often deeply intuitive, sometimes restless.

## Day Master reading

The water day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (water) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (巳) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 午, 未.
