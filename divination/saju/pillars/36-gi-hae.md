# 己亥 (기해) — Pillar 36 of 60

**Stem** — 己 기 (earth, yin)
**Branch** — 亥 해 (water, pig)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 壬, 甲
**Twelve-stage position** — 胎 태
**Nayin** — 平地木 평지목 (Plains Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 辰, 巳
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Soft earth meeting water — eroding gently. Compassionate, adaptable, sometimes self-effacing.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (亥) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 辰, 巳.
