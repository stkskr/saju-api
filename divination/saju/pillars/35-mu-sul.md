# 戊戌 (무술) — Pillar 35 of 60

**Stem** — 戊 무 (earth, yang)
**Branch** — 戌 술 (earth, dog)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 戊, 辛, 丁
**Twelve-stage position** — 墓 묘
**Nayin** — 平地木 평지목 (Plains Wood — wood)
**Empty branches** — 辰, 巳
**Stem-on-branch** — Rooted (same element)

## Character

Mountain on mountain — solid, unmovable. 괴강 pillar: tremendous willpower, can be stubborn. Often stands alone.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master is rooted in its own element — naturally strong. The pillar carries its own ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (戌) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 辰, 巳.
