# 甲戌 (갑술) — Pillar 11 of 60

**Stem** — 甲 갑 (wood, yang)
**Branch** — 戌 술 (earth, dog)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 戊, 辛, 丁
**Twelve-stage position** — 養 양
**Nayin** — 山頭火 산두화 (Mountainhead Fire — fire)
**Empty branches** — 申, 酉
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Tree rooted in dry mountain earth — must work hard to grow. Disciplined, principled, sometimes stubborn. Carries hidden fire and metal.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (fire) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (戌) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 申, 酉.
