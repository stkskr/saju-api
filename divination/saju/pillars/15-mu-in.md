**Stem** — 戊 무 (earth, yang)
**Branch** — 寅 인 (wood, tiger)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 甲, 丙, 戊

**Nayin** — 城頭土 성두토 (City Wall Earth — earth)

**Stem-on-branch** — Is controlled by (branch controls stem — pressure)

## Character

Earth on the wooded slope — under constant pressure from growth. Adaptable, defensive, sometimes overwhelmed. Hidden fire helps.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (earth) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (寅) is the spouse palace.
