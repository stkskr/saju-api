# 癸卯 (계묘) — Pillar 40 of 60

**Stem** — 癸 계 (water, yin)
**Branch** — 卯 묘 (wood, rabbit)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 乙
**Twelve-stage position** — 長生 장생
**Nayin** — 金箔金 금박금 (Gold-Leaf Metal — metal)
**Empty branches** — 辰, 巳
**Stem-on-branch** — Leaks (stem generates branch — stem element gives energy)

## Character

Spring rain on young growth — gentle nourishment. Kind, intuitive, often gifted in healing or teaching.

## Day Master reading

The water day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (卯) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 辰, 巳.
