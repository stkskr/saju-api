# 甲午 (갑오) — Pillar 31 of 60

**Stem** — 甲 갑 (wood, yang)
**Branch** — 午 오 (fire, horse)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 丁, 己
**Twelve-stage position** — 死 사
**Nayin** — 沙中金 사중금 (Sand-Buried Metal — metal)
**Empty branches** — 辰, 巳
**Stem-on-branch** — Leaks (stem generates branch — stem element gives energy)

## Character

Tree in summer fire — generation and consumption together. Passionate, generous, can burn out. Often charismatic.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (午) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 辰, 巳.
