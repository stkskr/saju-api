# 甲子 (갑자) — Pillar 1 of 60

**Stem** — 甲 갑 (wood, yang)
**Branch** — 子 자 (water, rat)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 癸
**Twelve-stage position** — 沐浴 목욕
**Nayin** — 海中金 해중금 (Sea Metal — metal)
**Empty branches** — 戌, 亥
**Stem-on-branch** — Is supported (branch generates stem — nourishment)

## Character

Tall straight tree rooted in clear water. Idealistic, dignified, drawn to growth and learning. Day master strongly supported.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master is nourished by the branch (water generates wood). Strong, well-resourced.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (metal) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (子) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 戌, 亥.
