# 戊子 (무자) — Pillar 25 of 60

**Stem** — 戊 무 (earth, yang)
**Branch** — 子 자 (water, rat)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 癸
**Twelve-stage position** — 胎 태
**Nayin** — 霹靂火 벽력화 (Thunderbolt Fire — fire)
**Empty branches** — 午, 未
**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Mountain reflecting in clear water — composed, observant. Wealth and emotion close together. Often financially capable.

## Day Master reading

The earth day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (fire) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (子) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 午, 未.
