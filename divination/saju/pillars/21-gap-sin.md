# 甲申 (갑신) — Pillar 21 of 60

**Stem** — 甲 갑 (wood, yang)
**Branch** — 申 신 (metal, monkey)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 庚, 壬, 戊
**Twelve-stage position** — 絕 절
**Nayin** — 泉中水 천중수 (Spring Water — water)

**Stem-on-branch** — Is controlled by (branch controls stem — pressure)

## Character

Strong tree being cut by axe — tension between growth and pruning. Resourceful under pressure, can produce great achievement through struggle.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (water) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (申) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 午, 未.
