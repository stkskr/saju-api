# 丁丑 (정축) — Pillar 14 of 60

**Stem** — 丁 정 (fire, yin)
**Branch** — 丑 축 (earth, ox)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 己, 癸, 辛
**Twelve-stage position** — 墓 묘
**Nayin** — 澗下水 간하수 (Valley Stream Water — water)
**Empty branches** — 申, 酉
**Stem-on-branch** — Leaks (stem generates branch — stem element gives energy)

## Character

Small flame on frozen earth — barely sustained warmth. Endurance under pressure, slow burn, can feel isolated.

## Day Master reading

The fire day master generates the branch — energy is expressed outward. Productive but can feel drained without other support.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (water) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (丑) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 申, 酉.
