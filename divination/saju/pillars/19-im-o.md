**Branch** — 午 오 (fire, horse)

**Nayin** — 楊柳木 양류목 (Willow Wood — wood)

**Stem-on-branch** — Dominates (stem controls branch)

## Character

Great water meeting summer sun — steam, contrast, drama. Charismatic, intense, lives big. Wealth easily attracted to this pillar.

## Day Master reading

The water day master controls the branch — capable of using its environment, but may feel constantly pushing against ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (午) is the spouse palace.
