# 乙亥 (을해) — Pillar 12 of 60

**Stem** — 乙 을 (wood, yin)
**Branch** — 亥 해 (water, pig)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 壬, 甲
**Twelve-stage position** — 死 사
**Nayin** — 山頭火 산두화 (Mountainhead Fire — fire)
**Empty branches** — 申, 酉
**Stem-on-branch** — Is supported (branch generates stem — nourishment)

## Character

Vine over flowing water — nourished but exposed to current. Romantic, sensitive, artistic. Day master well-supported.

## Day Master reading

The wood day master is nourished by the branch (water generates wood). Strong, well-resourced.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (fire) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (亥) is the spouse palace.
- 공망 for this 旬: 申, 酉.
