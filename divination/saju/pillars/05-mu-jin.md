**Stem** — 戊 무 (earth, yang)
**Branch** — 辰 진 (earth, dragon)
**Hidden stems in branch** — 戊, 乙, 癸
**Twelve-stage position** — 冠帶 관대

**Stem-on-branch** — Rooted (same element)

## Character

Mountain holding moisture and tree roots — fertile dam. Stable, patient, reliable. A 괴강 pillar (carries extra weight and authority).

## Day Master reading

The earth day master is rooted in its own element — naturally strong. The pillar carries its own ground.

## Notes

- This pillar's nayin element (wood) may differ from the stem and branch elements — useful for compatibility readings.
- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (辰) is the spouse palace.
