## Character

Iron in the forge — metal being shaped by fire. Decisive but at risk of burnout. Forceful personality, can be inflexible.

## Day Master reading

The metal day master is controlled by the branch — under pressure from its own ground. Capable of great achievement under stress.

- When this pillar is the Day Pillar, the branch (午) is the spouse palace.
