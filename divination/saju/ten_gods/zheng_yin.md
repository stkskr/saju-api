# 正印 (정인 jeong-in)

**Relation to Day Master** — produces Day Master; opposite polarity
**Category** — resources / nourishing

## Keywords
mother, education, support, tradition, knowledge

## Positive expression
Nurturing, well-educated, supported, calm under pressure, traditionally learned.

## Negative expression
Overdependence, slow to act, stuck in mother's orbit, paralysis from too much support.

## Life domain
mother, formal education, mentors, classical learning, support systems
