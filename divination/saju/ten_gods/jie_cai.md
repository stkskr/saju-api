# 劫財 (겁재 geop-jae)

**Relation to Day Master** — same element, opposite polarity to Day Master
**Category** — self / rivals

## Keywords
rival, robbery of wealth, ambition, competition, drive

## Positive expression
Drive, ambition, ability to act fast, charisma in competitive arenas.

## Negative expression
Reckless spending, jealousy, friends who take more than give, money slipping away.

## Life domain
rivals, half-siblings, competitive peers, fast money in and out
