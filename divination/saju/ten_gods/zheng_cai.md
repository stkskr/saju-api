# 正財 (정재 jeong-jae)

**Relation to Day Master** — Day Master controls; opposite polarity
**Category** — wealth / stable

## Keywords
earned wealth, salary, spouse (classically, wife), steady, careful

## Positive expression
Reliable income, careful with money, family-oriented, steady commitment.

## Negative expression
Stingy, conventional, fear of risk, calculating in relationships.

## Life domain
salary, savings, spouse (classically wife for a man), property
