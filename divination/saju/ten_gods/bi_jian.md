# 比肩 (비견 bi-gyeon)

**Relation to Day Master** — same element, same polarity as Day Master
**Category** — self / peers

## Keywords
siblings, peers, equals, competition, independence

## Positive expression
Self-confidence, independence, loyalty to peers, ability to stand alone.

## Negative expression
Stubbornness, isolation, ego competition, refusal to ask for help.

## Life domain
friends, siblings, business partners on equal footing
