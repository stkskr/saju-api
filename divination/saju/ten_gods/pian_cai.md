# 偏財 (편재 pyeon-jae)

**Relation to Day Master** — Day Master controls; same polarity
**Category** — wealth / unstable

## Keywords
wealth in motion, speculation, opportunity, generosity, deal-making

## Positive expression
Excellent at spotting opportunities, generous, charismatic with money, good with people.

## Negative expression
Speculation, infidelity (for men, classically), money in and out, scattered.

## Life domain
business, sales, deals, side income, father (classically)
