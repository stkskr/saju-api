# 正官 (정관 jeong-gwan)

**Relation to Day Master** — controls Day Master; opposite polarity
**Category** — authority / proper

## Keywords
proper authority, responsibility, career, spouse (classically, husband), honor

## Positive expression
Integrity, leadership through correct order, career success, honorable conduct.

## Negative expression
Rigidity, overconcern with status, constrained by duty.

## Life domain
government, corporate hierarchy, husband (classically for a woman), reputation
