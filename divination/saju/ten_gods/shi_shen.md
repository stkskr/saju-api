# 食神 (식신 sik-sin)

**Relation to Day Master** — Day Master produces; same polarity
**Category** — output / expression

## Keywords
expression, enjoyment, creativity, talent, nourishment

## Positive expression
Artistic expression, easygoing nature, ability to enjoy life and feed others, generative talent.

## Negative expression
Laziness, overindulgence, leaking energy without focus.

## Life domain
art, food, performance, kids (esp. for women), output of one's gifts
