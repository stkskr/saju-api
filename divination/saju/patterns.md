# Pattern theory (격국/格局)

The 격국 is the *structural shape* of a chart — what kind of bazi you're reading. Most charts fit one of eight normal patterns, derived from the dominant Ten God produced by the Month Branch's hidden stems.

## The eight normal patterns

The pattern is determined by which Ten God is produced when the Month Branch's primary hidden stem relates to the Day Master.

### 1. 정관격 (正官格) — Direct Officer pattern
The Month Branch produces Direct Officer to the Day Master. Indicates a structured, principled life path — civil service, law, structured careers. Best supported by 정인 (Direct Resource) and harmed by 상관 (Hurting Officer).

### 2. 편관격 / 칠살격 (七殺格) — Indirect Officer / Seven Killings pattern
The Month Branch produces Seven Killings. Aggressive, decisive, military or executive temperament. Needs taming — either by 식신 (Eating God) controlling it, or by 인성 (Resource) absorbing it. Untamed 칠살 is dangerous; tamed it produces extraordinary command.

### 3. 정재격 (正財格) — Direct Wealth pattern
Money, marriage (for men), steady accumulation. Best supported by 식상 (Output) and 관성 (Officer). Harmed by 비겁 (Peers stealing wealth).

### 4. 편재격 (偏財格) — Indirect Wealth pattern
Big money, big risk, big opportunity. Entrepreneurs, deal-makers, traders. Same support/harm structure as 정재격 but with more volatility.

### 5. 정인격 (正印格) — Direct Resource pattern
Learning, scholarship, mother, support systems. Best supported by 관성 (which generates the resource). Harmed by 재성 (Wealth) which controls Resource.

### 6. 편인격 (偏印格) — Indirect Resource pattern
Unconventional learning — esoteric, spiritual, technical, artistic skills. Can produce great originality or great instability.

### 7. 식신격 (食神格) — Eating God pattern
Creativity, productivity, enjoyment of life, children (for women). Generates wealth naturally. Harmed by 편인 which 'sees off' the Eating God.

### 8. 상관격 (傷官格) — Hurting Officer pattern
Brilliance, expression, performance, rebellion against authority. Often artists, performers, critics. Harmed by 정관 (Direct Officer) being present — clashes with the rebellion.

## Special patterns (특수격국)

When a chart doesn't fit normal patterns, look for these:

### 종격 (從格) — Follower patterns
When the Day Master is so weak it cannot stand on its own, it 'follows' the dominant force in the chart:
- 종재격 (follow wealth)
- 종살격 (follow killings)
- 종아격 (follow output)
- 종왕격 (follow peers — when same-element dominates)
- 종강격 (follow resource)

### 화기격 (化氣格) — Transformation patterns
When the Day Master combines with a stem and the entire chart supports the transformation, the Day Master *becomes* the new element. Five possible transformations matching the five stem combinations.

### 양인격 (羊刃格) — Goat Blade pattern
When the Month Branch is the Day Master's 羊刃 (peak yang energy branch). Powerful but volatile. Requires 칠살 (Seven Killings) to control.

### 건록격 (建祿格) — Established Prosperity pattern
When the Month Branch is the Day Master's 祿 (peer branch). Strong, self-supporting Day Master — natural leader.

## How to identify a pattern

1. Look at the Month Branch.
2. Identify its primary hidden stem (the strongest one, usually the first in the hidden stems list).
3. Determine what Ten God that hidden stem is to the Day Master.
4. That Ten God names the pattern.
5. Check whether the pattern is *intact* (the relevant element appears clearly elsewhere in the chart and isn't destroyed by clashes/combinations) or *broken* (compromised).

A clean, intact pattern produces a clear life direction. A broken pattern produces confusion or struggle.
